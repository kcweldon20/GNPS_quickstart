GNPS Analysis, Superquick Start


Drag and drop mzXML/mzML files here to quickly run a molecular network with up to 3 cohorts of samples. It is limited to 50 maximum spectrum files with each file limited to 200MB. Data uploaded will be removed on a monthly basis. Please create your own account for more persistent analyses.

NOTE: This interface is meant to process a small cohort of samples. If your internet is slow/unstable, we recommend the traditional method of uploading/running (see Documentation).
